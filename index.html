Game Overview:

NOTE:  Please play the tutorial for a more thorough explanation of the gameplay

In FTL you command a ship and her crew as it is being chased across the galaxy by the rebel fleet.  

The galaxy map is divided into Sectors and in each Sector are a number of Beacons (nodes).   You progress in this game by jumping from Beacon to Beacon, trying to get across the map to the the " Exit " which will let you jump to next Sector.   Each time you Jump, the rebel fleet advances closer to your position, so get to the exit as soon as you can.

At each Beacon, you will encounter an event which ranges from shopping at a store, to finding something useful, or fighting another ship.  

Get to the 8th , ‘final’ sector to defeat the Rebel Flagship and save the galaxy.

Controls:

Space Bar:       Pause the game – Commands and actions can be taken while paused.

Left Mouse Click:   Various uses

  1. Navigate Menus
  2. Turn on/Increase power to a system
  3. Select a crew member
  4. Turn on/Start to charge Drones & weapons
  5. Choose a target (by clicking on a room in an enemy ship after choosing the weapon)

Left Mouse Drag:   Select multiple crew members

Right Mouse :  Various Uses

  1. Decrease power to a system
  2. Move selected crew members

Keyboard "1-4":  Turn on/Activate weapons

Keyboard "5" & "6":   Activate Drones    

Keyboard "Escape":   Open up the menu
